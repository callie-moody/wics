Code to Inspire is the first coding school for women in Afghanistan. Fereshteh was born as a refugee near the border of Iran and Afghanistan after her family fled Afghanistan because of the Soviet invasion. A year after the Taliban regime collapsed, Fereshteh and her family moved back to Herat, Afghanistan. She earned her Bachelor in Computer Science and her Master's from Technical University of Berlin in Germany. She taught as a professor at Herat University for almost three years before founding Code to Inspire in 2015, which has already served around 300 girls
